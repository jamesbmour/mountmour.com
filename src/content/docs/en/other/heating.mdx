## Quick Guide

### Living Room and Kitchen
- The Heating Conditioner is to the left side of the stairs in the living room. Colored brown.
- The heating is done through the floor.
- The Heat is turned off in the summer and turned on in the winter.

### Bedroom
- The heating controler is located in each bedroom.
- There are different controlers for heat and AC so double check the labels
