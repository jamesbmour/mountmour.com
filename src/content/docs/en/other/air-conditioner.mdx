## Quick Guide

### Living Room and Kitchen
- The AC Conditioner is to the left of the TV in the living room
- The AC is turned off in the winter and turned on in the summer

### Bedroom
- The AC is located in the bedroom and is turned off in the winter and turned on in the summer
- There are different controlers for heat and AC so double check the labels
