## Quick Start Guide
- The washing machine is a washer and dryer combo.
- Do not put bulky items in the washing machine.
- Do not stop the washing machine mid-cycle or it will lock up and you will not be able to open it.

## Troubleshooting

### Washer is Locked up
- You need to flip the breaker to the washer and dryer to reset the machine.
- The breaker box is located to the left of the TV in the living room behind the picture frame.
- The breaker is labeled 
- Flip the breaker off for 2-5 mins and then back on to reset the machine.
