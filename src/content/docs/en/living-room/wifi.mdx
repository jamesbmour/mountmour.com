## Wifi Troubleshooting
If you're having trouble connecting to the WiFi, try these steps:
The Wifi router is located in the living room, to the right of the TV in the lower cabnet .

### Rebboot WiFi Router
1. Unplug the power cable from the WiFi router
2. Wait 10
3. Plug in the power cable again
4. Connect your device to the WiFi network (FH450)
5. Enter the password (Aspen123)
6. Try connecting again
